### What this FSI actually does (verified from company materials)

- **Not positioned as a franchise** (site states no franchise fees / no royalties); described as an **“FSI dealership”** — exclusive **protected territory** license to run a **mobile / on-site** commercial fleet service business.
- **Core service model:** Scheduled **preventive maintenance** plus breakdown/service calls at customer locations; reduces off-site shop downtime.
- **Capital guidance (site):** Typical total commitment about **$150,000–$500,000** (territory-dependent). Franchise listing sites cite **$100,000–$250,000** total investment and **$50,000** cash required (third-party, not audited).
- **Training & software:** Turnkey operations blueprint, vendor discounts (NAPA, AutoZone, CarQuest, etc. cited on franchise listings), proprietary dealer software.
- **Co-branding:** **Richard Petty / Petty’s Garage** partnership prominently featured ([Richard Petty advantage page](https://www.fleetservicesint.com/the-richard-petty-advantage)).
- **Corporate role:** Sells and supports **independently owned dealers** — dealers are “independently named, owned and operated” (franchise directory language).

- Franchise Solutions listing: [https://www.franchisesolutions.com/franchise/fleet-services-international](https://www.franchisesolutions.com/franchise/fleet-services-international)
